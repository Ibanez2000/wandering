Day trip from Taipei Yuanshan to Yangming Mountain 陽明山

2023/10/01

It's nice to get back on the bicycle when staying in Taipei for a few days. Luckily, there is a mountain range north of the city and from Yuanshan it's just 15 minutes to get there. I started the trip from the Corner Hostel at Yuanshan MRT station which is just next to 小角落青旅咖啡 Corner Hostel & Cafe where I stayed.

The route follows some local streets from Yuanshan MRT Station to Yuanming mountain.

I did not go very far and at the highest point the mountain was maybe 500m high. If you go further, it will go up to around 1.000m. The whole mountain range should give enough opportunities for bicycling two or three days.

Going up is following Yingdedalu 仰德大陸, a relatively busy mountain street with some buses and trucks.

Alternative streets suggested by a friend with less traffic:

Xingyilu 行義路 and Yangtougonglu 陽投公路.

Near Yingdedalu you have the opportunity to visit the absolutely stunning National Palace Museum. This museum is extremely significant because a huge percentage of Mainland China cultural treasures are kept here.

If going early, this route can be combined with a visit to the Taiwan National Palace Museum, my favourite sightseeing spot in Taipei.

Going down it is a fun side street which is pretty steep, lots of twists and hairpins. Best to be sure that the brakes are working properly!
